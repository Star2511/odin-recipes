Easy Chicken & Rice Casserole

Description

This dish is made with only 5 ingredients, and has juicy chicken breasts and tender rice in a delicious and creamy sauce. This is a casserole that packs a lot of flavour in a filling one dish dinner and a meal that everyone will love! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of uncooked white rice
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 1 package onion soup mix
  • 10 ounces of cream of mushroom soup condensed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. Spray 9×13 pan with cooking spray. Add chicken breasts and season with salt & pepper
  3. Pour uncooked rice over chicken. Sprinkle with onion soup mix
  4. Combine mushroom soup and 1 1/2 cups of water. Pour over chicken
  5. Cover and bake 1 hour 15 minutes or until rice is tender
